About MHHF

Inspired by Compassion, Driven by Impact

Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation (MHHF) is a wonderful organisation based in Ibadan, Nigeria, dedicated to supporting widows, orphans, and the less privileged irrespective of religion, tribe, or background - all inspired by the beautiful Islamic values of kindness, compassion, and charity.

Empowering Widows

Providing capital, skills, and support systems for widows to thrive with dignity.

Supporting Orphans

Offering educational sponsorship, emotional care, and shelter for vulnerable children.

Promoting Education

Ensuring access to school supplies, mentorship, and learning resources for young minds.

Providing Safe Water

Delivering clean water solutions that transform communities across Nigeria.
