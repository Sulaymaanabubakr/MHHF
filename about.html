About Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation

Our Story: From Vision to Reality

MHHF was born from a heartfelt mission to support widows, orphans, and the less privileged. What started as the Muslims Helping Muslims Initiative in 2020 has grown into a compassionate movement that serves everyone with dignity.

Founded in 2020 as the Muslims Helping Muslims Initiative, our organisation began from a deep desire to address challenges faced by vulnerable groups, particularly widows, orphans, and the less privileged.

In 2023, we officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as the Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation - expanding our vision to serve all, irrespective of religion or tribe.

Mission Statement

  • To establish and empower widows, orphans, and the less privileged.
  • To relieve needy families, the sick, and widows.
  • To provide clean and safe water for daily use.
  • To serve humanity with compassion and dignity.

Our Vision

To create a world where every widow is empowered, every orphan is supported, and every less privileged person has access to education, dignity, and opportunities for self-reliance - regardless of background.

What We Do

What We Do at Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation

Our humanitarian programmes span empowerment, relief, and long-term support initiatives designed to uplift widows, orphans, and less privileged families across Nigeria.

Feeding Project

We feed the widows, the orphans and the less privileged. By distributing foodstuffs to them. Helping them out in distress.

Empowerment Support/Programs

We empower the widows, orphans and the less privileged with business support, vocational training skills and business grants thereby making them self- sufficient.

Back To School Project

We donate educational materials to students who are orphans and Less privileged and we also help them cover their school fees. Making them participate in school activities. Thereby reducing the number of drop out students from Schools

Ramadan Feeding Project

We support and distribute raw foodstuffs to widows and less privileged families to ease fasting.

Zakatul Māl

We collect ZAKATUL MAL from eligible Muslims and help them give those who deserve it , we use such zakat to establish widows and struggling Muslims

Zakatul Fitr Project

We collect monetary value of zakat fitr from people and help them buy the Items (grains) and then distribute them to the right people (poor, widows,less privileged families) deserving the zakat fitr. Zakatul fitr is done every end of Ramadan. Where Muslims give desire grains of foodstuffs to the poor

Sadaqahtul Jāriyyah

Sadaqah jariyah (ongoing charity) is a charity whose reward continues after a person dies. we help people dig water projects to the community in need which serves as sadaqah Jariyyah for the person even after death. As Muslims, it's upon us to leave a project that will serve as a continuous charity for us. We can also build and supervise mosques projects that will continue as Sadaqahtul Jariyyah.

Help The Sick

We support the sick financially, help them cover up their medical bills.

Eid Clothing Project

We distribute new clothes to Orphans and less privileged children every Eid. We also distribute clothes and hijaab to widows and less privileged families to celebrate the occasion. We accept donations of neatly used clothes from donors and distribute them to those who are in need.

Eid-Ul-Adha Project (Meat Distribution)

We accept monetary donations to buy ram and raw rice and distribute to widows and poor families to celebrate EID-Ul-Adha.
