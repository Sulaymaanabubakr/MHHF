About Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation

Our Story: From Vision to Reality

MHHF was born from a heartfelt mission to support widows, orphans, and the less privileged. What started as the Muslims Helping Muslims Initiative in 2020 has grown into a compassionate movement that serves everyone with dignity.

Founded in 2020 as the Muslims Helping Muslims Initiative, our organisation began from a deep desire to address challenges faced by vulnerable groups, particularly widows, orphans, and the less privileged.

In 2023, we officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) as the Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation - expanding our vision to serve all, irrespective of religion or tribe.

Mission Statement

  • To establish and empower widows, orphans, and the less privileged.
  • To relieve needy families, the sick, and widows.
  • To provide clean and safe water for daily use.
  • To serve humanity with compassion and dignity.

Our Vision

To create a world where every widow is empowered, every orphan is supported, and every less privileged person has access to education, dignity, and opportunities for self-reliance - regardless of background.

What We Do

What We Do at Muslims Helping Humanity Foundation

Our humanitarian programmes span empowerment, relief, and long-term support initiatives designed to uplift widows, orphans, and less privileged families across Nigeria.

Empowerment & Vocational Training

We empower widows, orphans and the less privileged with business support, vocational training skills and business grants, making them self-sufficient.

Education Support

We donate educational materials to orphans and less privileged students and help pay their school fees, reducing school dropouts.

Food Relief Programmes

We feed widows, orphans and the less privileged by distributing foodstuffs to ease distress.

Ramadan Feeding & Support

We supply raw foodstuffs to widows and families to support fasting during Ramadan.

Zakatul Māl Distribution

We collect Zakatul Māl from eligible Muslims and distribute it to rightful beneficiaries, establishing widows and struggling Muslims.

Zakatul Fitr Distribution

We collect Zakat Fitr donations, buy grains, and distribute them to the poor, widows and less-privileged families at the end of Ramadan.

Sadaqah Jāriyah Projects

We help people sponsor water wells and mosque construction as Sadaqah Jāriyah, benefiting communities and donors after death.

Medical Assistance

We support the sick financially and help cover their medical bills.

Eid Clothing Distribution

We distribute new clothes to orphans and widows every Eid, including hijābs and garments from donated items.

Eid-Ul-Adha Support

We accept donations to buy rams and rice for widows and poor families to celebrate Eid-Ul-Adha.
